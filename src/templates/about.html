Synergy is a web based application that uses past data and analyses current trends to implement a dynamic pricing scheme on electrical energy.

It requires previous weather reports, and climatic information to forecast the weather and predict demand. This is then used to adjust the price during peak loads.

Customer informations such as income, square footage etc. is used to categorize them and rank the customers based on their energy consumptions.

Once the price is obtained, the system can help the user with his usage capacity.
